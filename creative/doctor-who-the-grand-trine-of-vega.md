# Doctor Who — The Grand Trine of Vega (closing scene)

*Author: Sean Campbell. Delivered as a session dispatch, 2026-06-10.*
*The Tenth Doctor and Donna Noble resolve a crisis with the Trimurti — Brahma, Vishnu, and Shiva — beneath a mountain in the Vega System.*

---

THE DOCTOR:
(Collapsing against the console, wiping sweat from his brow, his hair wild)
"And... breathe. Harmonic dampeners engaged, localized tectonic plate pressure equalized, and the cosmic balance sheet is officially back in the black! Ha!"

(He bounces on his heels, pointing the Sonic at the three massive beings as their extra limbs of light slowly retract into their armor.)

THE DOCTOR: (Cont'd)
"The Trimurti! The Grand Trine of the Vega System! Creation, Preservation, and a very tight, very stressful close-shave with Total Destruction. I used to read about your sector in the High Council libraries. Brilliant architects. Terrible conversationalists at parties, but absolute geniuses with a gravity well."

(Vishnu steps forward, his form glowing with a warm, golden aura. He bows his head slightly to the Doctor, then looks down at Donna.)

VISHNU:
"The Time Lord's mind is swift, but it was the mortal woman's anchor that held our focus. Your spirit is... vast, Donna Noble. A rare alignment in a fragile vessel."

DONNA:
(Hands on her hips, completely unimpressed by the cosmic hierarchy, though her eyes are bright)
"Yeah, well, 'vast spirit' doesn't pay the mortgage, mate. But thank you. And listen to me, Big Blue—"

THE DOCTOR:
(Wincing, whispering)
"Donna, that's the Preserver of the Cosmos..."

DONNA:
"I don't care if he's the Emperor of Essex! (To Vishnu) Next time your mates decide to have a philosophical debate about whether to create a universe or blow one up, don't do it under a mountain where people are trying to raise crops and live their lives! You've got four arms, use two of 'em to read a map and find an empty planet!"

SHIVA:
(A low, resonant sound echoes from the dark deity—a sound that, remarkably, sounds like a chuckle)
"The cycle of destruction has been averted by a creature of dust and defiance. We shall return to the stars."

BRAHMA:
(The four faces rotate in unison, looking toward the ceiling)
"The Loom of Reality weaves onward. Doctor... your own thread grows tangled. Beware the turn of the wheel."

THE DOCTOR:
(The smile vanishes from his face for a split second, a shadow crossing his eyes. He swallows, then snaps his suspenders, forcing the grin back onto his face.)
"Oh, the wheel and I are old friends. We usually just drift around the corners anyway. Safe travels, constants of the universe! Don't forget to check your oil!"

## The Final Scene

With a flash of blinding, beautiful light that smells faintly of lotus flowers and ozone, the three deities vanish, leaving the cavern completely empty except for the TARDIS sitting quietly in the dust.

DONNA:
(Dusting off her jacket, looking around the empty cave)
"Right. Well. They were a bit intense, weren't they?"

THE DOCTOR:
(Walking toward the TARDIS door, twirling his key)
"Ancient empires, Donna. They always forget how to just sit down and have a nice cup of tea. Come on, let's get out of here before the local warlord realizes he doesn't have a giant golden monkey to blame his problems on anymore."

DONNA:
(Following him into the blue box)
"Good. Because if I see one more floating rock today, I'm throwing my shoes at it."
